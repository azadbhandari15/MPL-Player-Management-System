# MPL-Player-Management-System
Player Management System for a Cricket Tournament Named MPL

It will consist of some use cases which will be used for managing a tournament with Auction Details it will consist of three major roles:
1. Admin - This role will be dedicated to commitee members here they can access the dashboard, payment details, budget owner details and player current status
2. Owner- This role will be dedicated to people who will be the captain or will be owning the team, they can see the player registered and can download the list
3. Player - This role will consist of checking their auction status
4. Permitted for All - Registration form will be accessible for all the people

## Use cases 
For MPL there will be multiple use cases which are defined as follows:
1. Player Registration
2. Find Player Details (If they were part of previous season their stats will be present as well)
3. List of Registered Players in the tournament
4. List of Players in the team
5. Live Auction Bidding
6. Auction History of the Player
